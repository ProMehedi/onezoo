ONEzoo Data Safety and Security

Keeping your data safe and secure is our highest priority.

How it Works

Striving For Industry Best Security

Data security is at the top of everyone's mind, and we are no exception. ONEzoo started in the cloud and all our services reside in the cloud. Because our business runs on the same technology, we are not only committed to protecting the data but taking proactive steps to mitigate risk for all of us.
We take your trust seriously and take great pains to ensure your data is kept in industry-leading conditions, and only availableto you and the people you allow.

Compliance is Key

We comply with and exceed industry standards when it comes to security. Credit Cards and high-level data is secured in PCI Compliant Servers(Hover: PCI), which has 12 levels of requirements to ensure information is protected. Your data is tokenised and stored in 256-bit encryption(hover:256). We utilise load balanced servers which spread the work across multiple servers in peak times, ensuring best performance 24/7. We have servers located across Australia to ensure we can offer the closest server to your business.
We have dedicated server management consultants who regularly perform stress tests and ensure we are running the latest security and server patches.
