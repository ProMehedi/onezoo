FAQ's

How does it work?

Once you sign up you'll see all the suppliers who provide to your area. You can then place an order and set a time to pick up or if the supplier provides delivery set a delivery day. Your order is guaranteed and we will facilitate a full refund if required. Please contact support for more help.

Who uses ONEzoo?

Banks, Businesses, and Boutiques. We have over 10 000 registered users in Australia encompassing a wide varied of industries. When you sign in you will see suppliers from a large range of industries.

What happens if there is an issue with my order?

Please contact support the moment you find any issues and we'll work with the supplier to remedy the issue or arrange a full refund.

I can't find the supplier I want?

Suppliers can make themselves visible to a niche group of customers, if you can't find the one you want, contact us and well help get you in front of them.
