Payment Credits

ONEzoo securely(link to security) stores credit card data which we can charge from the customer when they order, or at a specified delayed time. For example 7,14 or 21 days after the order is created or delivered.

Get started

How it Works

Outstanding Module

We have an outstanding module which you can use to manage your customers debt. We can send out regular statements and list the outstandings on the invoices to get the customers to pay on time.

Force Customers to Pay Via Credit Card if Their Outside Their Terms.

Force account customer outside of their terms to pay via credit card, this allows your customers to still place an order whilst they pay out their outstanding amount.
