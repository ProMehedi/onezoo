Get your drivers on a digital logistics solution

ONEzoo Drivers App allows your drivers to manage their delivery runs, mark off orders and take payment all on the go.

Get started

How it Works

See How Your Driver is Tracking

As your driver is progressing through the day, they can mark deliveries as paid and received. This allows you to see where the driver last was and importantly at what time. Think a driver is slacking off? You can now see how long between drops the driver takes.

Manage the Drivers Run and Set Triggers

Sorting paper invoices into an order is a thing of the past. Deliveries by ONEzoo allows you to drag and drop the orders into a run. You can also assign triggers to automatically assign orders to certain runs as they come in. For example, all orders in the suburb 3002 can be assigned to the city delivery run.
