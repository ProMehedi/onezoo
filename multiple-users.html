How it Works

Restrict What Your Staff Can Order

With ONEzoo you can apply restriction to users on your account such as restricting your venue manager from ordering from another supplier on the platform, or making it so your barista only orders the items set out in their product list. Restrictions can be placed on many more items, get in contact with us Call Now Send Message to find out more.

Let Your Accountant In

Your account needs access to all your purchases, with ONEzoo you can simplify the whole process. Just give them access to your account and they can log in at anytime and export your orders and begin preparing your BAS or general tasks
